Shape - Designing the Future of Music

Envisioning the future of music in 20 years, helping people answer the question, WHAT KIND OF MUSIC DO YOU LIKE?

Introduction

Shape is the answer to the question, What kind of music do you like? Your shape is the embodiment of your musical persona, a visualisation of your music listening habits and preferences. It makes use of your music history and your physiological responses to songs to create an easily shareable and relatable image.

Brief

We were collaborating with the California College of the Arts and Lawrence Azerrad to envision what the future of music would look like in 20 years. It was a deep dive into the music listening and sharing experience.
